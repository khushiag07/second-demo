National Robotics Championship

Date: Dec 10 – 12, 2025

Organized by: Indian Institute of Technology, Chennai

About

A three-day national-level robotics challenge where school students showcase their innovation in AI-powered robotics. The event includes workshops, competitions, and live demonstrations.

Highlights

  • AI and Robotics Hackathon
  • Drone Racing League
  • Robotic Arm Coding Challenge
  • Panel Discussions with AI Experts

Prizes

  • Winner: ₹50,000 + Trophy
  • Runner-up: ₹25,000
  • Participation Certificates for all
